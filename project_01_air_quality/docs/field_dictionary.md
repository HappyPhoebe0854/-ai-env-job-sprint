# Field Dictionary

## Purpose
This document explains the meaning, unit, and usage of each field in the Shanghai air quality dataset.

| Field Name | Meaning | Unit | Example | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| date | Observation date | YYYY-MM-DD | 2026-06-26 | Used for time series analysis |
| city | City name | Text | Shanghai | Used for city filtering |
| station | Monitoring station | Text | To be confirmed | Used for spatial analysis |
| AQI | Air Quality Index | Index | 85 | Main air quality indicator |
| PM2.5 | Fine particulate matter | μg/m³ | 35 | Important pollutant indicator |
| PM10 | Inhalable particulate matter | μg/m³ | 70 | Important pollutant indicator |
| SO2 | Sulfur dioxide | μg/m³ | 8 | Gaseous pollutant |
| NO2 | Nitrogen dioxide | μg/m³ | 40 | Traffic-related pollutant |
| CO | Carbon monoxide | mg/m³ | 0.8 | Gaseous pollutant |
| O3 | Ozone | μg/m³ | 120 | Often related to photochemical pollution |

## Notes
This field dictionary will be updated after the final dataset is selected.
